The Best Movies According to Ramis

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Pirates of the Caribbean

This is my favourite movie. It is funny, directed very carefully and with great cinematography.

Game of Thrones and House of the Dragon

These are my favourite series. They are about a medieval fantasy story with dragons and a fight for the throne.

Lilo and Stitch

This is my favourite animated movie. It is about an alien that accindently comes to Earth and got adopted by a cute little girl.
